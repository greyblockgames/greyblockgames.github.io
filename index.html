Who Are We.

Greyblock Games is based in Vancouver, BC, and was founded by Zachary Marra and Lukas Olson. Our team came about with an idea. This idea eventually became what is known as one of our current work-in-progresses, MexCube. Since then, we’ve got a little sidetracked, and have started developing other titles. GreyBlock Games is known for their contract work in the mobile games and interactive media industry but also has extensive experience in corporate software solutions.

Our Process.

Starting from an idea, we iterate over concepts until the solution comes naturally. Sure it may take longer than more traditional approaches to software design, but this is what we believe sets our products apart. All software is crafted with care, right here in Vancouver.

Our Approach.

Every project is a unique, living, iterative being that is unique to you. We put love into the works that we produce, ensuring that you receive exactly what you need. But we don't stop there, whenever your needs change, so does your software. And that's why we provide market leading support, no matter the project or size.
